Life Insurance, $600,000

This will provide enough funds to cover your mortgage debt and provide enough money to put in a trust for your little girl in the event of your premature death.

Trauma Insurance, Stand-Alone, $108,000 with early cancer payment option of $27,000

This is a tax free lump sum benefit that will be paid to you on the diagnosis of one of 42 serious conditions. This benefit will remove the financial worry that comes with serious illness allowing you to focus on your recovery. We have based this figure on one year of your gross salary. This will buy you some ‘financial stress free time’ whilst you are recovering from your illness.

Loan Repayment Insurance, $1,980 per month, 8 week wait, to age 65

This benefit will provide you with an income to meet your home loan repayments in the event of you being unable to work due to sickness or disability. This benefit is not offset by ACC payments. If included, this benefit may also be claimed in the event of you being made involuntarily redundant. The optional redundancy benefit has a 6 month stand down period and a 6 month benefit period.

Income Protection, $2,970 per month, Agreed Value, 8 week wait, to age 65

This benefit will provide you with an income in the event of sickness and disability and you being unable to work. This benefit would be in addition to the Loan Repayment Insurance covering your loan repayments and would provide you with an income to meet your living costs. Once we have acquired details of your existing Medical Assurance Income Protection policy we will ascertain whether it is prudent to replace it with a new Income Protection policy that is not cancellable.

Absolute Health with Specialists and Tests, $300 excess for your family

This benefit will provide you with a comprehensive Medical cover that will provide you with the ability to meet Specialists, Tests and Surgical costs.

[Wherever possible when multiple covers are required we try to recommend the same single company for all covers. We have found this creates “buying power” for our clients in the event of claims, as well as ease of underwriting. Obtaining covers from multiple companies requires providing each with their own set of medical requirements, plus you may also be required to pay multiple policy fees.]

[We also recommend that wherever possible all insurance contracts should include a Waiver of Premium benefit. Is there a free-look period?

All insurance policies have a minimum 14 day free look period, which gives the client time to read and understand their insurance policy wordings, terms and conditions and the right to cancel the policy without obligation if they change their mind or feel that the policy is not suitable for their needs.

We would advise you to read your policy documents very carefully and make a note of any items or paragraphs that are confusing or unclear. We will be happy to assist with further clarification to ensure you have a full understanding of your insurance cover.

What are the terms and conditions for fixed rate loans?

For each year of the fixed rate term, you can make extra payments, of up to 5% of the loan balance (at the beginning of the fixed rate term) or $10,000 whichever is the lesser. These payments can be in the form of regular payments or lump sums at no extra cost to you.
